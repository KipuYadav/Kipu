1. Introduction

Welcome to Kipu Fitness Guide! This course is built to transform your body, health, and mindset with practical knowledge. Whether you're just starting or want to level up, everything is covered here.

2. Benefits of Fitness

  • Improves energy and mood
  • Builds strong muscles and bones
  • Boosts confidence and discipline
  • Supports heart health and immunity

3. Fat vs Weight Gain

Weight Gain includes fat, muscle, water, and food weight. Fat Gain only means gaining body fat. Our goal should always be lean muscle gain, not fat accumulation.

4. Weight Gain Diet Plan

Eat calorie surplus food every 3 hours:

  • Morning: Banana shake + oats
  • Mid-morning: Peanut butter toast + almonds
  • Lunch: Rice, dal, paneer, salad
  • Snacks: Sprouts or protein bar
  • Dinner: Roti + sabzi + curd
  • Bedtime: Milk with whey protein

5. Weight Loss Diet Plan

  • Morning: Warm lemon water + oats + eggs
  • Lunch: Boiled veggies + roti + dal
  • Snacks: Roasted chana or fruit
  • Dinner: Soup + grilled paneer
  • Drink water and avoid sugar-rich foods

6. Home Workouts

Do 5 out of these 10 exercises per day:

  1. Push-ups (3 sets of 15)
  2. Jumping jacks (3 sets of 25)
  3. Squats (3 sets of 20)
  4. Lunges (3 sets each leg)
  5. Mountain climbers
  6. Planks
  7. Burpees
  8. Crunches
  9. Superman
  10. Wall sit

Train till muscle failure in last set. Rest 30-60 sec between sets.

7. Gym Workouts

Split training is best. Do 5 exercises per muscle:

  • Day 1: Chest + Triceps
  • Day 2: Back + Biceps
  • Day 3: Legs + Abs
  • Day 4: Shoulders + Traps
  • Day 5: Rest or cardio

Each exercise: 3-4 sets, 8-12 reps. Train till failure in the last set.

8. Workout Timings (Beginner & Advanced)

Best Time: Morning after light meal OR Evening before dinner.

Beginners: Train 1-2 muscle groups/day 4-5 times a week.

Advanced: Train 2 muscles/day 5-6 times/week + cardio.

9. Supplements & Tablets

  • Whey Protein: Muscle building
  • Creatine: Strength & pump
  • Multivitamin: Fill nutrition gaps
  • Omega-3: Heart & joints
  • Zinc, Magnesium, Vitamin D3: Boost testosterone

10. Testosterone

This hormone builds muscle, reduces fat, increases strength and libido. Sleep, lifting weights, and good fats (like nuts) help boost it.

11. Masturbation Awareness

Doing it once a month is normal and safe. Avoid frequent masturbation as it may reduce energy and focus during training.
