📘 Introduction

KIPU Fitness Guide aapko ek structured plan deta hai jisse aap weight gain, fat loss, aur muscle building jaise goals achieve kar sakte ho. Chahe aap gym jaate ho ya ghar pe workout karte ho, ye guide sab ke liye hai.

💪 Benefits of Fitness

  • Energy levels badhta hai
  • Immunity improve hoti hai
  • Muscle growth aur fat loss
  • Self-confidence boost

🍽️ Weight Gain Diet Plan (Veg)

  • Morning: Banana + soaked almonds + milk + whey protein
  • Breakfast: Paratha + curd + 4 boiled eggs (paneer if veg)
  • Lunch: Rice + dal + sabzi + 100g paneer
  • Pre-Workout: Banana + peanut butter toast
  • Post-Workout: Whey protein + 1 banana
  • Dinner: Chapati + paneer sabzi + salad
  • Before bed: Milk + dry fruits

🏠 Home Workout (No Equipment)

  • Push-ups – 3 x 15
  • Squats – 3 x 20
  • Plank – 3 x 30 sec
  • Lunges – 3 x 12 each leg
  • Mountain climbers – 3 x 20

🏋️ Gym Workout Plan

  • Day 1 – Chest + Triceps: Bench Press, Cable Fly, Tricep Pushdown
  • Day 2 – Back + Biceps: Lat Pulldown, Barbell Row, Dumbbell Curl
  • Day 3 – Legs: Squats, Leg Press, Calf Raises
  • Day 4 – Shoulders: Shoulder Press, Lateral Raise, Shrugs
  • Day 5 – Arms: Bicep Curl, Tricep Dips, Hammer Curl
  • Day 6 – Abs + Cardio: Crunches, Plank, Leg Raise
  • Day 7 – Rest

⚖️ Weight Loss Diet (Veg)

  • Morning: Warm water + lemon + soaked almonds
  • Breakfast: Oats + fruits + black coffee
  • Lunch: 2 chapati + sabzi + dal + salad
  • Snack: Roasted chana or fruit
  • Dinner: Veg soup + salad or 1 chapati + sabzi
