Beginners: Should train full body or 2 muscles per day. Example: Chest + Triceps or Back + Biceps. 3-4 times per week is enough.
Advanced: Can isolate 1 muscle group per day. Push-Pull-Legs or Bro Split works great. 5-6 days/week for better hypertrophy.

üìÖ Best Time for Workouts

  • Morning (6-8 AM): Great for cardio & fasted training.
  • Evening (4-7 PM): Best for strength training (Testosterone peak).
  • Night: Avoid heavy lifts. Do stretching, mobility, or walk.

üìä Calories & Diet Plan

Weight Loss: Eat less than TDEE. Keep 300-500 calorie deficit.
